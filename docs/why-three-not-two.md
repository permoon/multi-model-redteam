# Why three models, not two?

> 🚧 Under construction — built on Day 6.

This document will explain the design choice. Quick answer: with two
models, "consensus" and "unique" collapse into the same set; with three,
you get a meaningful gradient (3-way / 2-way / 1-way) that surfaces
blind-spot vs insight asymmetrically.
